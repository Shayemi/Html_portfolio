A drone is an unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV). There are two types of drone. They are

  1. Terrestial drones - This are the type of drone that perform their operations in the air. Types are Quadcopter drone and Fixed wing drone
  2. Aquatic drones - this are the type of drone that perform their operations underwater.
