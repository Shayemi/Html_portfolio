Adverbials Of Manner

Adverbials of manner tell us the way in which the action in the sentence was performed. Adverbials of manner answers the question how when put to the main verb in the sentence. They are introduced by words such as like, as, if, etc. Examples are underlined in the sentences below.

Examples are given below:

  1. The man ate the food as if he was going to die.
  2. The teacher flogged sade as if she was a thief.
  3. She told me how she killed the rat.
  4. He dressed like a king.
  5. You can't work like your father.
